Stick Kick

Welcome to Stick Kick

You are the red player and the computer is the blue player. You shoot first by selecting either left, right or middle. If you choose the same direction as the computer, the shot will be saved. If you choose a different direction, you'll get a goal.

After you kick, it's time to save. When saving you choose either left, right or middle again and the first to 5 goals wins.

Hit the reset button to restart the game at any point and the play/stop music button to toggle the game's music. If you need a refresh on the rules, then click the instructions button.
