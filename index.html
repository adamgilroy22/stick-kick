Stick Kick

YOU 0 - 0 CPU

Welcome to Stick Kick

The instructions are simple, you are the red player and the computer is the blue player.
You shoot first by selting either left, right or middle. After you kick, it's time to save.
When saving you choose either left, right or middle again and the first to 5 goals wins.

Hit the reset button to restart the game at any point and the music button to toggle the game's
music and sound effects. If you need a refresh on the rules, then click the instructions button.

It's your turn to kick
